 I recommend Thessaloniki, Greece. I recommend Thessaloniki because it has a rich history of historic marketplaces and bazaars. The city is known for its vibrant shopping scene, with many shops and markets selling traditional Greek goods, such as clothing, souvenirs, and local delicacies. The city's main shopping street, Tsimiski, is lined with shops like Zara and H&M, while the Proxenou Koromila and Mitropoleos streets offer a more local shopping experience. Additionally, the city's Modiano market and Kapani Market offer a wide variety of goods, including fresh produce, handicrafts, and souvenirs. The ideal month to visit Thessaloniki is July, as the city hosts the International Book Fair, which is a great opportunity to find unique and affordable souvenirs.
