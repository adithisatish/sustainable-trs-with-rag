Lyon is a city in France, known for its Renaissance architecture, museums, and gastronomy. Some of the must-see sights in Lyon include the Place Bellecour, the Basilique Notre-Dame de Fourvière, the Musée des Beaux-Arts, and the Musée d'Histoire de la Résistance et de la Déportation. Lyon is also known for its famous bistros, where you can try local specialties such as quenelles and croûtes gorgonzolas. The city has a rich history, and you can visit the Musée Gadagne to learn about Lyon's past. The city is also home to the Musée urbain Tony Garnier, which showcases the work of architect Tony Garnier.
