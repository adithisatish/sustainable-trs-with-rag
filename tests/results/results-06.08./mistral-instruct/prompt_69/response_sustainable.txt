I recommend Sibiu, Romania. Sibiu is a city with a rich cultural heritage and is known for its beautiful architecture, especially the Brukenthal National Museum, which is one of the most important museums in Romania. The city is also known for its festivals, including the Sibiu International Film Festival and the Sibiu Medieval Festival. Additionally, Sibiu has a vibrant nightlife scene, with many bars and clubs to explore. The city is located in the central part of Romania and is easily accessible by train or car.
