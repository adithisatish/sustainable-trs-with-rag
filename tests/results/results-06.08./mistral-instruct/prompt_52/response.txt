Based on the information provided, the best destination for a summer vacation in Europe that involves beaches, parks, and boat trips is Copenhagen, Denmark. Copenhagen has several beaches, including Atakum Beach, Fener Beach, and Bandırma Beach. The city also has several parks, including Atatürk Park, East Park, and West Park. Additionally, Copenhagen has a boat tour along the coast called the "Samsunum" that takes 2 hours and offers views of the city and the surrounding areas.
