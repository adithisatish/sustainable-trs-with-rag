I recommend Sibiu in Romania for your travel in spring. Sibiu is a city with a rich history and architecture, and it is home to many museums and landmarks that showcase the city's past. The city has a unique blend of German and Romanian culture, which can be seen in the architecture, food, and traditions. Additionally, Sibiu is known for its beautiful parks and squares, making it a great place to explore on foot. The city is also known for its craft and souvenir shops, which offer a variety of unique items to take home. Overall, Sibiu is a great destination for anyone interested in ancient history and architecture.
