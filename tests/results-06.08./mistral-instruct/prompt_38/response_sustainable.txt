Based on your preferences, the best place to go on a weekend trip in June would be Copenhagen, Denmark. Copenhagen is known for its beautiful architecture, charming canals, and lively cultural scene. There are many things to do in the city, such as visiting the famous Little Mermaid statue, exploring the charming neighborhood of Nyhavn, and visiting the Tivoli Gardens amusement park. Copenhagen is also a great place for foodies, with a wide variety of restaurants and cafes serving delicious Danish and international cuisine. Additionally, Copenhagen has a well-connected public transportation system, making it easy to get around and explore the city.
