I recommend Strasbourg for your romantic anniversary trip in November. Strasbourg is a charming city with a rich history and beautiful architecture that is perfect for a romantic getaway. The city center is pedestrian-friendly and can be explored on foot or by bicycle. With its stunning views of the Rhine River and its many historic landmarks, Strasbourg is sure to impress. Additionally, Strasbourg is home to the European Parliament, making it a unique and interesting destination for history and politics enthusiasts. The city's many shops, restaurants, and cultural attractions make it a great place to spend a few days exploring.
