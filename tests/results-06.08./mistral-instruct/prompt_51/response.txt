There are many hotels in Amsterdam. Some of them are budget hotels and some are luxury hotels. The prices for a budget hotel room can range from {{EUR|18}} to {{EUR|100}} per night, depending on the time of year and the quality of the hotel. Luxury hotels can cost anywhere from {{EUR|150}} to {{EUR|400}} per night. The best time to visit Amsterdam is in the spring and autumn, when the weather is mild and the crowds are smaller. The most popular areas to stay in Amsterdam are the [[Amsterdam/Binnenstad|Binnenstad]], [[Amsterdam/Zuid|Zuid]] and the [[Amsterdam/Jordaan|Jordaan]]. The [[Amsterdam/Red Light District|Red Light District]] is a popular area to stay in, but it is not recommended for families or those who are uncomfortable with the local culture.
