 I recommend Varna is a city in Bulgaria. I recommend Varna because it is the best option for a beach holiday with lots of water sports in July. The city has a beautiful beach with a lively nightlife, and it offers various water sports such as surfing and kite-surfing in the bay. Additionally, there are several surf and sea sports schools, which you can find on the North Beach. You can also book diving and visit the nearby St. Anastasia island, which is visible from any point from the Burgas beach. The city hosts the Bourgas Sand Fest from the beginning of July until the end of August, which is a festival of sand sculptures. Overall, Varna offers a perfect combination of beach activities and water sports in July.
