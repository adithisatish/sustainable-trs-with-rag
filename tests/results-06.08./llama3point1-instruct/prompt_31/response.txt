 I recommend Stavanger, Norway for your trip in January. Stavanger has a maritime climate with cool summers and mild winters, which is ideal for winter sports. You can enjoy snow-boot hiking, skiing, or ice skating on Stokkavannet in the winter. The city also offers beautiful scenery and a range of outdoor activities such as hiking, climbing, and island hopping. The nearby mountains offer excellent opportunities for skiing and snowboarding, and the city's proximity to the coast provides a unique combination of mountain and sea landscapes.
