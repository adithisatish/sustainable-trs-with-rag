 I recommend Sibiu, Romania, for your interest in ancient history and archaeology. Sibiu is a city with a rich history dating back to the 12th century, and it has preserved many of its medieval and Baroque architectural styles. The city has a strong cultural heritage, with numerous museums, galleries, and historical landmarks, such as the Brukenthal Museum, which houses a vast collection of artifacts and exhibits. In the spring, the city's historic center is particularly beautiful, with blooming flowers and greenery. The city's proximity to the Carpathian Mountains and the Transylvanian Plateau also offers opportunities for hiking and exploring the surrounding region. Overall, Sibiu is an ideal destination for those interested in ancient history and archaeology, with its rich cultural heritage and natural beauty.
