 I recommend Porto, Portugal. Porto is a city with a rich cultural heritage, and its art galleries and exhibitions are a major draw for visitors. October is a great time to visit Porto, as the weather is mild and there are many festivals and events taking place. The city's art scene is thriving, with numerous museums and galleries showcasing the work of local and international artists. The Ribeira district, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is a must-visit, with its colorful buildings, picturesque streets, and lively atmosphere. The city's many museums, such as the National Museum Soares dos Reis and the Serralves Museum, offer a glimpse into Porto's rich cultural and artistic heritage. Additionally, the city's historic center is home to many beautiful churches, monasteries, and other architectural landmarks. With its unique blend of Portuguese and European influences, Porto is a city that will leave you enchanted and inspired. I recommend Porto as the best city to visit in October for art lovers.
